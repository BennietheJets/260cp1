Passion Pit

Bio

Formed in Cambridge Massachusetts Passion pit consists of Singer/Keyboards Michael Angelakos,
Guitar/Synth Ian Hultquist, Thom Plasse on Bass, and Adam Levinsky on Drums. Manners was released
in 2009. Through the years the members of the band have changed but Michael Angelakos has remained
the frontman and writer for the band through it all. Their most popular song "Take a Walk"
was released in 2012. Though they have lost general popularity through the years they had a
very successful 10 year anniversary tour for their Manners album in 2019.
