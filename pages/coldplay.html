Coldplay

Bio

Coldplay consists of Singer Chris Martin, Guitarist Jonny Buckland, Bassist Guy Berryman, and Drummer William
Champion. Originally calling themselves Starfish for 1 day Coldplay started at University College in London in 1996.
Their first ablum was released in 2000. Their fame quickly spread worldwide with several tours and new albums.
Most famous songs include "Viva la Vida", "Paradise", and "A Sky Full of Stars". The album Mylo Xyloto was written
as a musical with the two main characters being Mylo and Xyloto. Ghost Stories was written after the divorce of Chris Martin
and Gwenyth Paltrow. William Champion guest starred on Game of Thrones as a drummer in a band there too.
