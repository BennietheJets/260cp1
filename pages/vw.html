Vampire Weekend

Bio

Vampire Weekend origianally consisted of Singer and Guitarist Ezra Koenig, Drummer Chris
Tomson, Bassist Chris Baio, and multiinstrumentalist Rostam Batmanglij. Their self-titled
album was released in 2008 and was renowned for its influences from styles of music around
the world. Between their third and fourth albums Batmanglij departed the band to follow his own
projects. There was a large amount of time between their third and fourth albums. They spent years
recording and perfecting each song on the fourth album. Their highlight song from the ablum
"Harmony Hall" they spent over a year on alone.
